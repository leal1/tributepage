About Arnold

  • Born on July 30th, 1947 in Graz, Austria to Aurelia Jadrny and Gustav Schwarzenegger.
  • Arnold was Inspired by American Olympic swimmer Johnny Weissmulle to become an athlete
  • Started his bodybuilding carreer determined to win the title of Mr. Universe
  • In 1961, Arnold met Kurt Marnul, former Mr. Austria, and trained with him at Athletic Union in Graz
  • In 1964, Arnold's father tells him he can only go to the gym three times a day, so he builds his own home gym
  • In 1965, Arnold won the bodybuilding competition in Struttgart and awarded Jr. Mr. Europe
  • In 1966, Arnold wins: The Best Built Man in Europe, Mr. Europe, and the International Powerlifting Championship.
  • By the Age of twenty, he was named Mr. Germany and Mr. Universe
